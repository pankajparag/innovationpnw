The FACC PNW High Tech & Innovation Committee is back with another exciting event! The Committee organizes an event on the theme:  Start-up in technology founded or led by Frenchmen in the Pacific Northwest. Questions related to the creation of a startup will be addressed. Presentations will be in French.

THEME: Startups WHERE: SURF Incubator Address:

SURF Incubator
999 Third Ave Suite 700
7th Floor
Seattle, WA 98104

WHEN: Thursday, December 8, 2016 from 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM (PDT)

Parking will be FREE.

Food and drinks will be provided. It is an amazing opportunity to learn more about startups, but also to network and meet people from various sectors!

HiTech Innovation Committee INNOVATION PNW

The FACC High-Tech and Innovation Committee is a group established by the French-American Chamber of Commerce of the Pacific Northwest (FACC PNW).

Its purpose is to create an active network of French-speaking individuals (“Franco-phones”) and French / American companies sharing a common interest in High-Tech and Innovation
